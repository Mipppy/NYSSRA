Club Championship Series

NYSSRA Nordic's Cross Country Clubs compete all season long for the right to be named NYSSRA Nordic Club Champion. The Club Championship Series is based on the NYSSRA Cross Country Race Calendar and features meet scoring, with clubs earning points during each race block.

Points are awarded in 10 racing blocks throughout the season, culminating with the NYSSRA-Nordic Champions Cup weekend. The club accumulating the most points will be crowned the "NYSSRA-Nordic Club Champion" and will receive the Club Championship banner.

Race Blocks

  • Block 1: December Racing
  • Block 2: January First Weeks
  • Block 3: January
  • Block 4: Empire State Games Weekend
  • Block 5: February First Weeks
  • Block 6: February End
  • Block 7: NYSSRA Championships - Men's Relay
  • Block 8: NYSSRA Championships - Women's Relay
  • Block 9: NYSSRA Championships - Mixed Relay
  • Block 10: NYSSRA Championships 20 km Skate

Points Scoring

Points will be scored using the NYSSRA-Nordic points system. Clubs earn points based on the total points scored by their members during each event. The club with the most points will be named “Event Champion” and awarded 20 points towards the overall club series. Other clubs receive points based on the following scale:

FAQs

Can any club participate? Yes, as long as they are sanctioned by NYSSRA.

How do clubs score points? Club members score NYSSRA series points during races. Total points are calculated for each club after every race.

Who can join a club? Anyone with a NYSSRA membership can join a club and represent it during races.

When are awards presented? At the conclusion of the NYSSRA Champions Cup Race.

Eligibility

  • Clubs must be NYSSRA-Nordic affiliated before scoring points.
  • Club members must have a current NYSSRA-Nordic membership and designate their club affiliation on their membership form and race registration.
